Tips for posting in Poolii

So you've won the lottery and now have the priviledge to post to the Poolii community! Congratulations! Although this is exciting, for some people it is intimidating and overwhelming. What do you say? Here are some tips that might help you have your say.

  • Be authentic - Say something about what is going in your life right now. It doesn't have to be exciting or dramatic. People love getting insight into other peoples lives, no matter how exciting or mundane.
  • Share your opinion - Tell the Poolii community what you think about anything you want be it politics, food, people, current events in the news or anything else you want. As long as it is not hateful, you will be heard and respected.
  • Share a link - If you want people to check out your business, sign a petition, or view your latest photo include a link in your post to get more engagement.
  • Share a quote - Are you inspired by a specific quote, or something someone said to you in the past? Share it with us!
  • Add social links to your profile - Go to your profile and add social links where Poolii users can connect with you after they've read your post.
  • Recommend a book - Share a book with a short description of how it may help people. Let us know why you liked this book.
  • Recommend a food - What is your favorite tea or healthy snack? Do you enjoy a particular peppermint tea source? Let everyone know.
  • Recommend an app - There are so many apps out there right now for mental health, mindfulness, productivity, fun and more! What apps are useful to you?
  • Recommend another business or healing practitioner - Do you know a great massage therapist in your area? What about a really awesome eating disorder specialist? Let everyone know!
  • Share an insight - What type of amazing things do you know or have you discovered? Can you share an insight?
  • Share your approach - Maybe you’re an expert in something! What insider research do you have access to? Let us know what’s going on and your unique take on it.
  • Post a link to a services page - What services do you offer and to whom?
  • Tell a short, entertaining story - Some of the most shared content on the internet is simply entertaining.
  • Share an organization - Is there an organization that you like to volunteer with or donate to? Tell us about a cause you care about and why.

Hope this helps a bit!
